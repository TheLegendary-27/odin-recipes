Savory Gravy

Mouth-watering KFC-style Gravy

Learn how to create this simple, yet flavourful condiment to add to your favorite dishes. It's surprisingly easy even a non-cook can do it. This KFC-style gravy is a dream for those who want to indulge, so follow along as this guide will tell you how to step-by-step.

Ingredients

  • Pint of boiling water
  • 2 chicken stock cubes
  • 1 beef stock cubes
  • 1 tbsp of dried sage
  • 3 tbsp of butter
  • 3 tbsp of white flour

Steps

  1. Put the chicken cubes and beef cube into the boiling water. Give it a mix until dissolved
  2. Add in the dried sage. Mix.
  3. Preheat your pan.
  4. Put butter on pan. Melt it and move it around to coat pan.
  5. Once all butter is melted, put in the white flour. Stir around to combine the flour and butter to make a roux. Don't overcook the roux, we're looking for golden brown.
  6. Add in half of stock mixture into the roux. Stir well until it slightly thickens.
  7. As the mixture thickens more, gradually add the rest of the stock. Keep stirring well until desired consistency is achieved.
  8. Serve.
